Direct types, are all the types from the Templar quadra, and all the ones who have [[Extroverted Thinking|Te]] as their Hero or Parent function.

Direct types are: [[ESTJ]], [[ESTP]], [[ENTJ]], [[ENFJ]], [[ISTJ]], [[INTP]], [[INTJ]] and [[INFJ]]
